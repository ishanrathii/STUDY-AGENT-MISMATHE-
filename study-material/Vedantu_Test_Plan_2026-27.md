## MISMATHE reading of this plan

1. **Every RT has a second life.** RT-02, RT-05, RT-08, and every CT repeat within 1–2 weeks. Strategy: sitting 1 = honest diagnostic (send MISMATHE the debrief) → targeted drills → sitting 2 = lock the marks.
2. **Cumulative tests are the real bosses.** CT-01 (23 Aug) already covers 7+ chapters. Nothing can be "done and forgotten" — this is why the spaced-revision blocks exist.
3. **Big gaps are gift weeks.** 6 Sep → 4 Oct and 22 Nov → 13 Dec are ~4-week gaps: perfect for backlog clearing, deep weak-area repair, and getting ahead.
4. **March 2027 = three full-length tests in 8 days.** The whole year builds to that fortnight.
