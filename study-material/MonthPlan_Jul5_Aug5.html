The two non-negotiables

📲 The Daily Recap — 9:45 pm, every night, to MISMATHE

  1. Exercise: done ✅ / scaled ⚠️ (what you did)
  2. Studied: which blocks happened, which chapters/topics
  3. 3 learnings: one line each — forces your brain to consolidate
  4. 1 confusion: the thing that didn't click (I fix it in my reply)
  5. Ratings: focus /10 · energy /10 · hours studied

Takes 3 minutes. I reply with the fix for your confusion + tomorrow's ONE thing + schedule adjustments. Miss a night? Send it next morning — the streak survives, silence doesn't.

🏋️ The Exercise Contract — 7:15–7:55 am, daily

  • It happens before the phone, before books, before everything. First win of the day, every day — test days, mock days, holidays, bad days.
  • What counts: run, cycle, skipping, bodyweight circuit (push-ups/squats/plank), sport — anything that raises your heart rate for 30+ min.
  • Sick or dead-tired? It scales, never cancels: minimum version = 15-min brisk walk + 10 push-ups + 1-min plank.
  • Why it's here: daily exercise = better focus, lower stress, deeper sleep — it literally buys back more marks than the 40 minutes it costs.

Ground rules

Coaching-sync rule Vedantu's live topic always wins — if they're on a different chapter, your next Block 1 follows THEM. Self-study supports coaching, never fights it.
Phone protocol Another room during every CORE block. No phone for the first 30 min after waking and the last 30 before sleep.
Protect the core Bad day? Keep exercise + Block 1 + Block 2 + recap. Drop the FLEX block guilt-free. Never lose two days in a row.
Sleep floor 7 hours minimum. Sleep is when today's study becomes memory — cutting it is un-studying.
⭐ doubt log Every question you can't crack gets a star and comes to the nightly recap. Nothing stays confusing longer than 24 hrs.
No-shame clause Missed blocks are data, not sins. Tell me in the recap and we re-plan. The past is dead; the streak restarts instantly.
